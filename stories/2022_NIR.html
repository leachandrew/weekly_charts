Canada’s 2022 National Emissions Inventory

Andrew Leach


Canada’s National Inventory Report (NIR) and emissions projections (PDF) were released last week, and along with the Emissions Reduction Plan (ERP) released a week earlier, dramatically updates the picture we have of Canada’s emissions through the first months of the COVID pandemic, and also gives us a sense of just how far we have to go to meet our targets. I’ve updated most of my NIR data page which gives you a group of data visualizations you are more than welcome to use. I hope you enjoy it.
